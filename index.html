About Us

B.R. Damodaren M.A., B.L.

B.R.Damodaren M.A., B.L. is an indian politician and also the state president of samajwadi party in tamilnadu state. He is very active in social reforms and social welfare. He has fighting for the welfare for poor peoples.

Damodaren, born on May 16, 1973. He is an advocate and also a Boxer National Player. He later turned in to full time politicians. Inspired by samajwadi party movements in tamilnadu, he joined Samajwadi Party. And now he is the State President of Tamilnadu. Damodaren is also a State General Secretary of Tamilnadu Yadava Maha Sabha.
